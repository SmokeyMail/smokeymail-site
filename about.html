About Smokey Mail

Founded with a deep commitment to digital privacy and a love for the serene beauty of the Great Smoky Mountains, Smokey Mail emerged from the belief that everyone deserves a secure and private space for their online communications. In an era dominated by data harvesting and intrusive practices, we stand as a beacon of trust and reliability.

Our mission is simple: to provide an email service that prioritizes your privacy above all else. We are a U.S.-based company, independent from big tech conglomerates, ensuring that our operations are transparent and our focus remains solely on protecting your data. We don't scan your emails, we don't sell your information, and we don't compromise on security.

Just as the Smokies offer a sanctuary of natural beauty, Smokey Mail aims to offer a digital sanctuary for your most important conversations. Join us in reclaiming your digital freedom.
